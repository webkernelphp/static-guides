# 01 - Webkernel for Enterprises & Institutions

## You should own your systems

Many organizations are forced to choose between:

- Expensive custom development (slow, expensive, risky)
- Foreign SaaS (convenient until it isn't — lock-in, data residency, pricing changes, disappearance)
- Half-baked internal tools that never quite work

Webkernel offers a third path.

## What you actually get

Install once. Then add the capabilities you need as real modules.

- Multi-panel backoffice ready from day one
- Proper access control and business scoping built in
- Module system so different teams (or partners) can deliver features without breaking everything
- Full source code ownership. Standard PHP stack (Laravel, Filament, Composer). No magic runtime.
- You can host it yourself or through Numerimondes Cloud / partners. No vendor lock-in.

## For governments, ministries, and large institutions

You need durable systems that can outlive any single vendor or administration.

Webkernel is designed so that:

- The core platform is stable and versioned together
- Modules follow strict contracts
- You can bring in local developers or partners to extend it
- You control the data and the infrastructure

## For companies

You don't need to hire a 15-person dev team just to have an internal ERP or client portal that actually matches how you work.

A small internal team + the right modules (built by you or by a partner) can go very far.

## The spectrum

- Non-technical teams can create forms, workflows, and datasets without writing code.
- Technical teams can go deep and write full modules when the domain requires it.
- Everything stays inside the same coherent platform.
